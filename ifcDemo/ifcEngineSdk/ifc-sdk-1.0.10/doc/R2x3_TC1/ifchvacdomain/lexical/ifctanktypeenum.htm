IfcTankTypeEnum

Definition from IAI: Enumeration defining the typical types of tanks. The IfcTankTypeEnum contains the following:

  • PREFORMED: Tank is preformed.
  • SECTIONAL: Tank is sectional.
  • USERDEFINED: User-defined tank type.
  • NOTDEFINED: Undefined tank type.
  • EXPANSION: Tank is an expansion tank.
  • PRESSUREVESSEL: Tank is a pressure vessel.
HISTORY: New enumeration in IFC 2x2.

EXPRESS specification:

TYPE IfcTankTypeEnum = ENUMERATION OF
( PREFORMED,
SECTIONAL,
EXPANSION,
PRESSUREVESSEL,
USERDEFINED,
NOTDEFINED);
END_TYPE;
